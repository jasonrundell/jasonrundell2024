Do / Don’t

Do

  • Prove leadership and craft in the first viewport
  • One job per section
  • Match Line-Art Pencil mocks after approval
  • One accent focal mark per illustration

Don’t

  • Purple gradients, dark glow, cream + terracotta clichés
  • Resume-page or pure IC portfolio vibes
  • Hero overlays, pill clusters, hero stat strips
  • Multiple accent fills in one illustration
  • Implement before explicit approval
