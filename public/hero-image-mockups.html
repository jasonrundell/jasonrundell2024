Guide amendment — required only for Track B
Proposed

Imagery → add a “Portraiture” rule

The Imagery section currently says the dominant visual is continuous-line editorial illustration and that no photography is required in the hero. Track B needs that softened to a single sanctioned portrait, with the illustration system still owning interior sections:

  • One portrait, one place. A single approved portrait of Jason; the line-art system remains the dominant visual for every other section.
  • Grade: desaturated ~70%, slight contrast lift (grayscale(.72) contrast(1.04)) — never a full $accent duotone flood, which would break the one-focal-mark rule.
  • Frame: hairline $lineSubtle, sharp corners. No arches, rings, radii, or offset shadows.
  • No overlays. No text, badges, chips, or numerals on the image. Captions sit outside the frame in normal flow.
  • No decorative brass. Brass stays the craft signal (proof figures, craft notes, tech-stack lines, tags) — never a tick, crosshair, or ornament around the photo.
  • Motion: portrait fades in with the hero copy; no parallax, no draw effect. Honours prefers-reduced-motion.
